About Us

Welcome to **Hostel Scouts**, your trusted platform for finding the best hostels near Manipal University Jaipur (MUJ). As **MUJ students**, we built this website to **help fellow students** easily explore available hostel options.

We aim to provide **reliable information** on **hostel availability, facilities, ratings, and pricing** so that students can make informed decisions.

Our platform is constantly improving, and we welcome feedback to enhance the experience for all students.
